# Mission Established: CTO-Level Mastery Across Three Pillars

Tom's goal is not just interview prep — it is to become a genuine CTO: architect-level system thinking, senior coding craft, and expert problem solving. He is already functioning as CTO at applyGOAT but recognises that execution ability is ahead of conceptual depth. Teaching should build both simultaneously.

**Implications:** Every lesson should serve one of three pillars (Architect Knowledge / Senior Coding / Problem Solving) and connect to his real projects. The zone of proximal development starts just above what he's already done in production — not at the very basics.
